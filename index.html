Executive Summary

The current model proposes an optimized framework for micro-loans that addresses critical constraints: Pure Cash Flow scoring without credit bureau reporting.

Key Findings

  • 4-tier system with graduated limits
  • Cash Flow Risk Score (CFRS) weighted model
  • Behavioral incentives program
  • Cross-sell strategy for profitability

Strategy Requirement

Lending alone is NOT profitable without bureau reporting. Requires 60%+ cross-sell adoption (account fees, interchange, bill pay) for overall profitability. Breakeven expected at month 15-18.

Cash Flow Risk Score (CFRS)

Since we don't use credit bureaus, the model is 100% based on observed cash flow behavior in the MyBambu account.

CFRS = (Cash Flow Capacity × 0.50) + (Transaction Pattern × 0.30) + (Account Behavior × 0.20)

1. Observed Income Stability (OIS) - 50% Weight

Inferred income from regular deposits in the account.

Base OIS = (Avg Monthly Deposits / $500) × 100
Adjustments: +15 regular deposits, +10 multiple sources, -20 high volatility, -30 few deposits
Score Range Monthly Deposits Pattern Risk Level
85-100 $600+ Very stable, regular Low Risk
70-84 $400-600 Stable Moderate
50-69 $250-400 Irregular Elevated
0-49 <$250 Insufficient High Risk

2. Cash Flow Coverage Ratio (CFCR) - 30% Weight

Can the balance cover loan payment + essential expenses?

CFCR = (Min Balance Projected - Loan Payment) / Essential Expenses
CFCR > 2.0 = 100pts | 1.5-2.0 = 85pts | 1.0-1.5 = 70pts | 0.7-1.0 = 50pts | <0.7 = 30pts

3. Transaction Velocity & Consistency (TVC) - 20% Weight

Frequency, volume, and consistency of account activity.

Condition Adjustment
Account active >40 days last 60 +15
80+ transactions in 60 days +10
Balance never reached $0 +10
Less than 10 active days -20
Balance at $0 for 7+ days -25
Dormant account (activity only for application) -30
